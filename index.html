The Art of Communication

Greetings & Ice-breaking

💬 Dialogue script:
“Hello, nice to meet you! I’m from [country] and I just arrived last week.”
“Welcome to China! Let me know if you need any help.”

✨ Tip: Briefly share your purpose (study/exchange) to build rapport.

Asking for Help

“Excuse me, sorry to bother you. I don't know how to ______. Could you help me?”
✅ Always say “Xiè xie (谢谢)” after being helped.

Ordering Food

“It’s my first time here, could you recommend some classic dishes?”

Key phrase: Diǎn cān (点餐) – order food.

Social Taboos & Etiquette

  • ❌ Avoid asking income, age, marital status, religious beliefs.
  • 🤝 Light handshake is enough – no hugging/cheek kissing generally.
  • 🙏 Never directly reject kindness; express gratitude first.
  • 🥢 Use serving chopsticks on formal occasions.

Common Chinese Phrases (pinyin + English)

Nǐ hǎo (你好) – Hello Xiè xie (谢谢) – Thank you Bù kèqi (不客气) – You're welcome Duìbuqǐ (对不起) – Sorry Dǎrǎo yīxià (打扰下) – Excuse me Zàijiàn (再见) – Goodbye Diǎn cān (点餐) – Order food

Smart Mobility & Transportation

Amap (高德地图)

Search destination → routes with real-time traffic, cost estimates. English navigation, ride-hailing & ticket booking.

Subway (Metro)

✅ Ticket vending machines (English) or Alipay Travel Code. Hours 6:00–23:00, avoid rush hours 7-9am & 5-7pm.
🔄 Free transfer at interchange stations.

City Buses

💰 Cash (exact change) or Alipay/WeChat QR code. Bilingual stops, wave to signal driver. Sightseeing buses available.

Didi (滴滴)

Enter destination → choose "Express" for best value. ⚠️ Verify license plate before getting in. Night surcharge for remote suburbs.

High-Speed Rail

Book via 12306 app (English, passport registration). Arrive 40 min early. Second-class best value, free hot water onboard.

Healthy Eating: Nutritious Chinese Dishes

Steamed Perch (清蒸鲈鱼)

Cantonese classic – steamed with ginger & scallion. Tender, low fat, high-quality protein, easy to digest.

Kung Pao Chicken (宫保鸡丁)

Famous Sichuan dish: diced chicken with peanuts, chili. Sweet, sour, salty, mildly spicy – foreigner-friendly.

Shrimp with Scrambled Eggs (虾仁滑蛋)

Mild flavor, fresh shrimp + fluffy eggs. Low fat, high protein, suitable for all ages.

Scrambled Eggs with Tomato (番茄炒蛋)

China's iconic home dish. Tangy tomato + soft eggs, rich in lycopene & protein. Perfect with rice.

Hot Pot (火锅)

Social dining: choose broth (spicy, bone, mushroom) and cook meat, veggies, tofu. Endless fun.

Sweet & Sour Pork (糖醋里脊)

Crispy pork strips in glossy sweet-sour sauce – loved worldwide, bright fruity flavor.

Mapo Tofu (麻婆豆腐)

Spicy Sichuan tofu with minced beef, numbing & aromatic. Light calories, budget-friendly, great with rice.

Restaurant Selection & Dining Tips

  • 📍 Open Amap “Secret Food Ranking” – popular local spots.
  • 🗣️ Ask residents for authentic hidden gems.
  • 👥 Choose crowded restaurants → fresh and tasty.
  • 📝 Read diners' reviews & real dish photos before ordering.
  • 🏬 Chain restaurants guarantee hygiene & consistent quality – ideal for newcomers.
  • ⚠️ Avoid unlicensed street stalls if you have a sensitive stomach.
  • 🍜 Traditional snack streets → sample multiple local bites in one place.

Life in the Digital Age: Essential Apps

WeChat

Bind intl credit card → pay everywhere. Mini-programs for taxi, food delivery, translation. 🔐 Enable 2FA, avoid unknown links/red envelopes.

Alipay

Visa/Mastercard binding, travel code, currency converter. 🔐 Avoid public Wi-Fi for large payments, activate account insurance.

Amap

English navigation, real-time traffic, ride-hailing. Share live location with friends for safety.

Meituan

Food delivery, groceries, medicine. English address input. Inspect delivery & protect privacy (use building address only).

Google Translate

Voice & camera translation, offline Chinese pack. Never upload passport/bank info for privacy.

General digital safety: Download apps from official stores, enable transaction alerts, never share SMS verification codes. Freeze payment apps immediately if phone lost.
